Q: 
Imagine that you are hired to enable data analytics;
where would you start? How would you go about gathering requirements?
Hint: Think backward from what the business may need. 

A:
I presume that in order to enable data analytics, I would need to collect
large amounts of data to be analyzed.
The first step would be to perform a data strategy on how to collect
and securely store data.
This data would then need to be extracted, transformed, and loaded.
through a pipeline into a data warehouse where it could be analyzed,
This data would also need to be tended to with the monitoring.
of the pipeline, by means of scheduling, executing, and monitoring.
A company could also employ ML to improve their pipeline.
workflow and data.
Ultimately, this data would then be served to the end user.
by means of dashboarding and APIs.
